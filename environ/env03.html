In the very back of a closet was an old dusty dollhouse. It was originally owned by a girl named Sam Beth who had long forgotten it. Spiderwebs grew across the tiny wooden bed and mold crept across the floor. The doll family still lived happily though, tucked away in their own world, safe from humans. The dolls have now become good friends with the spiders and live a happy communal lifestyle. Originally stuck with the same diet of plastic salad and soup, the Dolls have now branched out from their vegetarian lifestyle to enjoy the occasional spider-caught fly. Additionally, the spiders now recline outside of their webs and enjoy a neatly made, grandma-crafted doll bed.

Where are all our friends? It is meal time!
